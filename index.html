Rapunzel

About Rapunzel

  • A dreamer and a brave princess
  • Has magic hair that can heal injuries and make people youthful again
  • She finds the courage to leave her tower one day in search of the lanterns that fly every night on her birthday.

Rapunzel as she leaves the tower for the first time


Character Development

Stage 1:Before finding her courage

  • Spends the whole day doing various activities in her tower
  • Loves to paint
  • Plays with her pet chameleon, Pascal
  • Listens to her mother, Mother Gothel ,and obeys her

Stage 2:Her adventure

  • Manages to strike a deal with a stranger thief, Flynn Rider, to take her to see the lights
  • Runs into dangerous situations but always managed to think quickly on her feet and escape
  • Meets Maximus a royal palace horse who joins them for the adventure
  • Mother Gothel tries to convince her to come back to the tower but she stands up for herself
  • Falls in love with Flynn

Stage 3:Discovering herself

  • Gets tricked into thinking that Flynn betrayed her and goes back to Mother Gothel
  • Discovers that she is the princess
  • Demands answers from Mother Gothel but gets trapped instead
  • Flynn tries to save her and gets injured in the process
  • Rapunzel manages to defeat Mother Gothel
  • Reunites with her parents and lives happily ever after!
